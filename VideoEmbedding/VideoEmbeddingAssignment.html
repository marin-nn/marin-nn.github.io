How to Embed a Video into a Website

Step 1: select your video

Choose any server for hosting videos and either find a video that allows embedding or upload it on your own.

Step 2: the share button

Now that you've picked your video click the share button which is usually under the video.

Step 3: embed

A small menu will pop up. Click on the "embed" button: it's the first one in the list.

Step 4: copy the link

Whatever service you chose it will generate the code on it's own. You just need to click the "copy" button or copy this text manually.

Step 4.1: optional settings

You can also change how your video is displayed and choose the time at which it will start automatically below.

Step 6: paste the link

Now enter the programm you use for coding and simply past the link. Congrats, the video is now embedded on your website!
