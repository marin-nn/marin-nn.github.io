Welcome!

Taekwondo is originally a martial art which was developed during the 1940s and 1950s by Korean martial artists with experience in martial arts such as karate and Chinese martial arts. In the early 1970s, a new style of Taekwondo was developed to promote it as a competitive sport, alongside its traditional martial arts form. Taekwondo is a martial art sports that trains trainees to execute bare-handed defense and attack techniques characterized by different types of kicks for the purpose of self-defense and self-realization. This website is entirely dedicated to taekwondo. Here you can learn a lot about this amazing martial art. Whether you want to read about the different techniques used in taekwondo or learn more about its history, you can do it all here. Even if you know a lot about taekwondo, you might be able to discover a new side of it.
