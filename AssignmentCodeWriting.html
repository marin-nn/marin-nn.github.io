Welcome to Genatsvale and Khinkali! We are a team in love with Georgian cuisine. You will find both authentic dishes cooked according to traditional recipes, from delicious khinkali to flavorful shish kebabs, and author's dishes of modern youth Georgia.

We have created a cozy atmosphere for a comfortable stay with friends or family. We also offer convenient delivery so that you can enjoy our dishes in the comfort of your home.

We invite you to discover new flavors of diverse Georgia!

Genatsvale&Hinkali in the Obolon district of Kiev is a restaurant serving Georgian family cuisine, which pleases you with delicious food and an unforgettable atmosphere.
